Grovix

Grovix is a full-stack AI-powered agriculture platform designed to empower farmers and streamline agricultural trade. The platform provides weather intelligence, AI-based crop disease detection, market insights, and a direct farmer-to-buyer marketplace, helping reduce dependency on intermediaries and improve transparency in the agricultural ecosystem.

✨ Key Highlights
AI Crop Disease Detection
Real-Time Weather Insights
Direct Farmer-to-Buyer Marketplace
Market Price Analytics
Government Scheme Updates
Multi-Language Support
Secure & Transparent Trading
🎯 Objective

To build a digital agriculture ecosystem that helps farmers make data-driven decisions, increase profitability, and connect directly with buyers through technology.
